# Code of Conduct

This project is intended to be a respectful, constructive place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and discuss the code, not the person.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Accept correction gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or insults
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith engagement

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or block participation when behavior makes the project harder or less safe to maintain.

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately rather than escalating in public.
